Update Theme → Auto Update

In this guide you will learn how to update the theme using the Envato Market plugins . This plugin lets you easily update from the WordPress admin, without manually upload any files.

Step 1: Navigate to Envato Market from your WordPress admin.

Step 2: Go to tab Setting
